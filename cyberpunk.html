Cyberpunk 2077: Phantom Liberty

Review by Ahmed • Apr 15, 2025

Ahmed

Cyberpunk Specialist • 500+ hours in Night City

"The future is now, choomba."

The Redemption Arc

After three years of updates and improvements, Cyberpunk 2077's Phantom Liberty expansion delivers on the game's original promise...

What Works

  • Completely revamped skill trees with meaningful choices
  • Gritty spy-thriller storyline with Idris Elba's standout performance
  • Vehicle combat and police systems that finally work as intended

Final Verdict

Phantom Liberty isn't just a great expansion - it fundamentally improves the entire Cyberpunk 2077 experience. This is the game we should have gotten in 2020, with Night City finally feeling alive and dangerous in all the right ways.
